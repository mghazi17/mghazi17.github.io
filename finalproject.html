Final Project

The final project I programmed is a simple yet joyus and fun take on the classic snake game we know and love
As a player you can select the level of difficulty! Hard being for brave and courageous
This version of the snake game also allows you to choose which theme you want your snake to be from!
The snake itself is my bitmoji and the body is depends on the setting, like stars, water drops or tiny trees
Just select a difficulty and a theme, then use the arrow keys to move the snake and grab as much food as you can
